# 03 — Your First Session

This guide gets you playing something within five minutes. No building required — use a ready-made Content Pack.

---

## Step 1: Open the app

Start your local server and open the app in Chrome or Edge. You should see the session designer with an empty timeline.

---

## Step 2: Load a Content Pack

Click the **Content Packs** button in the left sidebar (or press the grid icon). You'll see six ready-to-run sessions:

| Pack | What it does | Length |
|------|-------------|--------|
| **Classic Induction** | Gradual depth induction with calm→build→peak→recovery arc | 5 min |
| **Conditioning Foundation** | Attention-responsive training with reward/redirect rules | 4 min |
| **Partner Introduction** | Gentle intro designed for use with a partner present | 4 min |
| **Solo Surrender** | Escalating solo session with adaptive intensity | 5 min |
| **Grounding Reset** | Calming, stable session for re-centering | 3 min |
| **Spiral Descent** | Visualization-heavy induction with spiral animation | 4 min |

Choose **Classic Induction** to start. It's the clearest example of how the session arc works.

---

## Step 3: Look at what loaded

After loading, the sidebar shows the session structure. You'll see:

- **Blocks** — the timed content items (text overlays, TTS prompts, pauses)
- **Scenes** — four phases: Settle, Drop, Depth, Rise
- The **timeline** at the bottom shows duration

The inspector panel on the right shows details of whatever you click.

---

## Step 4: Press Play

Press **Space** or click the ▶ play button.

The screen goes into **fullscreen playback mode**. The text blocks appear at their scheduled times. The background dims. If you have a device connected, it responds.

**During playback you can:**
- Press **Space** to pause/resume
- Press **N** to skip to the next scene
- Press `[` and `]` to reduce or increase intensity by 10%
- Press **ESC twice** for immediate emergency stop

---

## Step 5: After the session

When the session ends, a **post-session summary** appears showing:
- Total duration
- Attention quality
- XP earned
- Any achievements or quests completed

Your profile panel (click the avatar icon in the top bar) tracks cumulative progress.

---

## Step 6: Try a second pack

Load **Spiral Descent** to see visualization blocks in action — the screen shows an animated spiral during playback in addition to the text overlays.

---

## What to try next

Once you're comfortable with playback:

- **Customize a pack:** Click any block in the sidebar → change its text in the inspector → press Save (Ctrl+S)
- **Add a background track:** Drag an audio file onto the sidebar's audio section
- **Try a different mode:** Settings → Session Mode to change how the session behaves
