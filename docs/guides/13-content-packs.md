# 13 — Content Packs

Content packs are complete, ready-to-run sessions. Load one to start immediately without building anything.

---

## The six packs

### Classic Induction
**Duration:** ~5 minutes | **Scenes:** Settle → Drop → Depth → Rise

The reference induction. Four scenes following the classic calm→build→peak→recovery arc. TTS narration guides the induction. No device required, but works well with one.

**Good for:** First sessions, induction practice, demonstrating the platform.

---

### Conditioning Foundation
**Duration:** ~4 minutes | **Scenes:** Calibration → Training → Integration

An attention-responsive conditioning session. Three active rules reward sustained focus and redirect on drift. Includes a looping training scene that runs until integration.

**Good for:** Behavioral training, attention development, structured conditioning.

---

### Partner Introduction
**Duration:** ~4 minutes | **Scenes:** Welcome → Connect → Practice → Close

Designed for a two-person setup. Gentle, clear pacing. The operator can use live controls while the subject follows the session. No harsh transitions.

**Good for:** First sessions with a partner, introducing new participants to the format.

---

### Solo Surrender
**Duration:** ~5 minutes | **Scenes:** Release → Descent → Hold → Return

Escalating solo experience. Intensity builds progressively. Designed to be run without operator intervention — the session drives itself.

**Good for:** Experienced solo practitioners, exploring adaptive escalation.

---

### Grounding Reset
**Duration:** ~3 minutes | **Scenes:** Arrive → Ground → Settle → Complete

A calming, restorative session. Low intensity throughout, slow pacing, Recovery-type state for most of the session. Good as a cooldown after intense work.

**Good for:** Post-session recovery, centering before or after other activities, low-energy days.

---

### Spiral Descent
**Duration:** ~4 minutes | **Scenes:** Focus → Deepen → Spiral → Surface

Visualization-heavy induction using the spiral and tunnel viz blocks. TTS narration synchronized to visual transitions.

**Good for:** Practitioners interested in visual induction, hypnotic depth work.

---

## Loading a pack

Click **Content Packs** in the sidebar, or open the **Packs** tab in the inspector. Click any pack to load it.

> **Note:** Loading a pack replaces the current session. The replacement is undoable (Ctrl+Z) and the previous session is also auto-saved.

---

## Modifying a pack

After loading, everything is editable:
- Click any block to change text, timing, or type
- Add or remove blocks
- Change scene boundaries
- Adjust or remove rules
- Export as your own `.assp` file

Content packs are starting points, not locked templates.
